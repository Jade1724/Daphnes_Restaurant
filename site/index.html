How We Started

Established in 2010, our aim is to offer the Christchurch community a wide variety of authentic Chinese cuisine and a memorable dining experience.

At Daphne’s we use only the finest fresh seasonal ingredients sourced locally and around New Zealand to provide high quality Chinese food and this is reflected in the great success that Daphne’s Restaurant is receiving.

For your next dining experience join us at Daphne’s Restaurant for an authentic taste of Chinese cuisine.
